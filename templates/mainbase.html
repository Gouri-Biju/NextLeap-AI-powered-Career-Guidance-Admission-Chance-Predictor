About This Platform
🎓 Career Guidance & College Admission Chances Prediction

This AI-powered platform helps students make informed academic and career decisions. It allows colleges to manage requests and parents to track their children's academic journey.

🔍 Key Features:
  • 🧠 Career assessment through an intelligent chatbot-based test.
  • 🎯 Personalized college, course, and job recommendations.
  • 📈 Admission chance prediction using academic history and real-time datasets.
  • 📬 Communication portal for students, colleges, and parents.
  • 📰 Educational articles and updates to guide decisions.
👥 Who Can Use This?
  • Students: For test-based guidance, suggestions, and admission prediction.
  • Parents: To view their child's progress, suggestions, and admission status.
  • Colleges: To manage datasets, admission history, and course requests.
  • Admins: To manage users, colleges, complaints, and send updates.
💡 Why Use This Platform?

Choosing the right academic path can be difficult. This system uses real-time data, college profiles, and student test results to provide clear, reliable, and personalized recommendations.
